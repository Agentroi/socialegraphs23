<p><b>Ariie West</b> (born Robert Nicholson Salik on April 4, 1999) is an American musician, songwriter, and producer from Harlem, New York.<sup id="cite_ref-auto_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_career">Early life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ariie_West&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and career"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>West began his career at the age of 15 under the guidance of music executive Jay Jackson, also known as Ice Pick of Ruff Ryders. He was signed and assigned to create songs for Teyana Taylor, under Pharrell's label, Star Trak Entertainment.<sup id="cite_ref-auto_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career_breakthrough">Career breakthrough</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ariie_West&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career breakthrough"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2016, West met music producer John Kwatakye-Atiko, marking a turning point in his career. Their professional collaboration officially began in October 2022.<sup id="cite_ref-auto_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In January 2023, West released his single "Dangerous", which garnered 1.7 million views within its first two weeks on YouTube.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
